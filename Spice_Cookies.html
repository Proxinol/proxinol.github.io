Spice Cookies

Servings: 18
Calories: 200kCal/Cookie
Prep: 24+ hours
Cook: 30 minutes
Cooling time: 40 hours
Ready in: 26ish hours

Ingredients

Cookies

  • 2 1/4 cups all-purpose flour (scoop and level to measure)
  • 2 tsp baking soda
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1 1/2 tsp ground cinnamon*
  • 1 tsp ground nutmeg*
  • 1 tsp ground ginger*
  • 1/2 tsp allspice*
  • 1/2 tsp ground cloves*
  • 1 cup light-brown sugar
  • 1.5 sticks of butter
  • 1 egg
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1/3 cup molasses

Instructions

  1. Mix wet ingredients, beat until combined while scraping down the sides of the bowl.
  2. Mix dry ingredients into the wet until just combined.
  3. Cover the dough in plastic wrap and chill until at least the next day.
  4. Cookies are about 2 Tbsp each.
  5. Bake at 375F until cracks are visible along the top of the cookie, 11-13 minutes.
  6. Let cookies soldify on sheet for 2 min before transferring to cool completely.

Notes

*You can adjust the amount of spices used here to suit your taste. The dough is freezable.
